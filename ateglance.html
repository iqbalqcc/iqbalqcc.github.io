About HPL HR

We recognize that our employees are the key to our success and nothing can be achieved without their engagement. The long-term success of the Company depends on its capacity to attract, retain and develop employees able to ensure ongoing and sustainable growth. The Human Resources (HR) structure enables and empowers them in establishing business needs and their corresponding people requirements. Therefore, the mission of HR is to provide professional guidance to line managers aiming to deliver superior business results by optimizing the performance of our people, while ensuring exemplary working conditions. We are committed to providing our employees good working conditions, a safe and healthy work environment that support a better balance of personal and professional life. Learning is part of the Company culture. Employees at all levels are systematically encouraged to consider how they upgrade their knowledge and skills. At HPL, a high performance culture supported by differentiated rewards and development is key to the delivery of individual and business objectives. This is driven by the alignment of clear and challenging responsibilities and ensuring that employees are aware of how their work impacts HPL. A dynamic organization creates a climate of innovation and allows people to think from different perspectives. At HPL we encourage our people to take risks. Mistakes may be made but there is always a willingness to correct and learn from them.
